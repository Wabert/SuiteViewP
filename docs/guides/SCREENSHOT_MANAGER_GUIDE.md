# Screenshot Manager Guide

## Overview
The Screenshot Manager is a powerful tool for capturing, organizing, and exporting screenshots directly from the SuiteView application.

## Features

### Capturing Screenshots
1. Click the **Grab** button in the top-left corner of the app bar
2. The window will minimize and capture the entire screen
3. The screenshot will appear as a thumbnail in the "Screen Shots" panel on the left
4. Screenshots are automatically numbered and timestamped (e.g., "1 14:30")

### Viewing Screenshots
- Click on any thumbnail in the left panel to view the full screenshot in the Viewer panel on the right
- The image will automatically scale to fit the viewer while maintaining aspect ratio

### Managing Screenshots

#### Renaming
1. Right-click on any thumbnail
2. Select **Rename**
3. Enter the new name and press OK

#### Deleting
1. Right-click on any thumbnail
2. Select **Delete**
3. Confirm the deletion

#### Reordering
- Click and drag thumbnails to reorder them in the list

### Selecting Screenshots
- Click a thumbnail to select it
- Ctrl+Click to select multiple thumbnails
- Shift+Click to select a range of thumbnails

### Exporting Screenshots

#### Export to Word
1. Select one or more screenshots (click to select, Ctrl+Click for multiple)
2. Choose **Word** from the combo box at the top-right
3. Click the **Export** button
4. A new Word document will open with:
   - Minimal margins (0.5 inches) for maximum screenshot space
   - Each screenshot's name as a heading
   - The screenshot image below the heading
   - Spacing between screenshots
   - Images automatically scaled to fit the page width

#### Export to Outlook
1. Select one or more screenshots
2. Choose **Outlook** from the combo box at the top-right
3. Click the **Export** button
4. A new Outlook email will open with:
   - Each screenshot embedded in the email body
   - Screenshot names as headings above each image
   - Proper spacing between screenshots
   - Ready to add recipients and send

## Tips
- The Export button is only enabled when you have selected at least one screenshot
- Screenshots persist during your session - they're not lost if you minimize or hide the window
- The window can be resized; the viewer will automatically adjust to show screenshots at the optimal size
- All screenshots are captured at full screen resolution and only scaled for display

## Technical Notes
- Requires Microsoft Word for Word export functionality
- Requires Microsoft Outlook for email export functionality
- Both applications must be installed on your system
- Screenshots are captured from the primary monitor
