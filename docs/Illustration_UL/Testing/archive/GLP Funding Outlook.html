Coverage & method

  • 1 policies fully analyzed of 17,324 in scope. 4 bypassed (MD: 2 · rates missing: 2 · no solution: 1); 17,319 not yet run.
  • All forecasts run on a guideline-only basis (Conform to TEFRA on, Conform to TAMRA off) from each policy's inforce values as of its valuation date.
  • Projected lapse holds the current billing premium and mode; a bridging lumpsum is applied when the policy would otherwise lapse before its next due date.
  • Level prem to maturity is the minimum level modal premium that keeps the policy in force to maturity, allowing GLP exception premiums where required.
  • Exceptions required means even maximum guideline-allowed funding (premiums accepted up to the limit every month) fails to carry the policy to maturity.
  • Source: GLP Limit Calc v2.xlsx, produced by the SuiteView illustration engine's GLP forecast batch.
